• Measure your arm span and compare it to your height
  • Arm span is distance between the tips of your middle fingers
  • When measuring your arm span place your back against a doorpost
  • Remember that Ape index can be positive or negative. If you have a longer arm span than height you are positive and if it's less it's negative
  • When you have figured out the difference divide it by two to find out the ape index. For example a person who is 200 cm tall and have a arm span of 210 cm has an ape index of 10 cm.
