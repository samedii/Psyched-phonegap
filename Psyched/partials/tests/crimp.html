Warm up your fingers properly!
  • For this you need strips of different sizes with sharp edges, a transgression board is recommended
  • Half crimp, don't full crimp (i.e. no thumb on index finger)
  • Mark down the smallest size you can hang on for 10 seconds and still feel like you could have kept going for at least another 2 seconds
